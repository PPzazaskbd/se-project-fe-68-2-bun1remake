## 🍪 Cookie Policy

**Last updated: April 24, 2026**

At **Bun1 Hotel Booking**, we believe in being clear and open about how we collect and use data related to you.

### **How We Use Cookies**

We use cookies to enhance your experience, keep our services secure, and help us understand how people use our site:

2.  **Performance Cookies:** These help us count visits and traffic sources to measure and improve performance.
3.  **Functional Cookies:** These enable enhanced functionality like remembering your preferences.

### **Your Choices**

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.
